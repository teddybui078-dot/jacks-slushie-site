Our story

Made by Jack, approved by a dolphin

Jack started spinning slushies from a beach cart with one blender and zero chill. His buddy Finn the dolphin taste-tests every batch. If Finn does a backflip, the flavor makes the menu.

Maximum brain freeze, minimum frowns

Every cup is layered by hand with real fruit and a ridiculous amount of love. We keep the ice fluffy, the colors loud, and the brain freeze strictly optional.
